Calo Meal Plans Overview

Calo offers personalized meal plans designed based on your goals, preferences, and lifestyle. Meals are prepared by chefs and designed by nutritionists. Meals are delivered fresh daily and are ready to eat.

Available Meal Plan Types:

Balanced Plan:
A well-rounded meal plan that provides all essential nutrients needed for general health and daily energy.

High Protein Plan:
Focused on increasing protein intake to support muscle growth, strength, and recovery.

Low Carb Plan:
Low in carbohydrates and higher in healthy fats and vegetables. Suitable for weight loss and low-carb lifestyles.

Vegetarian Plan:
Plant-based meals made with vegetables, grains, and vegetarian-friendly ingredients.

Chef’s Picks Plan:
A selection of meals designed for taste and variety rather than strict nutritional goals.

Custom Macros Plan:
Allows users to define their own macronutrient targets (protein, carbs, fats). Designed for athletes and advanced users.

Meal Customization:

Customers can choose:
- Breakfast
- Lunch
- Dinner
- Snacks

A minimum of 2 meals per day is required, including at least lunch or dinner.

Subscription Options:

Customers can select how many days per week they receive meals. A minimum of 5 days per week is required.

Billing Options:
- Weekly
- Monthly
- 3-month subscription

Pricing:

Meal plans start from approximately:
- BHD 3 per meal
- BHD 2 per breakfast

Key Features:

- Meals are personalized based on fitness goals such as weight loss, muscle gain, or maintenance.
- Customers can choose meals and swap dishes they do not like.
- Over 80 meal options are available each week.
- Meals are prepared with fresh, natural ingredients.
- Customers can exclude allergens or unwanted ingredients.
- Plans are flexible and can be paused, skipped, or modified.

Delivery:

- Meals are delivered daily.
- Meals are fresh and ready to eat (just heat and eat).
- Changes to delivery or meals can typically be made up to 24–48 hours in advance.

Flexibility:

- No long-term contracts.
- Customers can cancel anytime.
- Plans can be adjusted based on changing goals or schedules.
