Calo Policies and Terms

Subscription Policy:
Calo operates on a subscription-based model. By subscribing, customers agree to recurring charges based on the selected plan (weekly, monthly, etc.). Subscriptions continue automatically until paused or canceled by the customer.

Cancellation Policy:
Customers can cancel their subscription before the first delivery if they contact support before the order is dispatched. After the first delivery is confirmed, cancellations are not allowed, but subscriptions can be paused instead.

Customers may cancel shortly after placing an order (in some cases within a limited time window). After that, cancellation may not be possible for that delivery.

Pause and Skip Policy:
Customers can pause their subscription at any time through the app, as long as the next order has not already been dispatched.

Customers can:
- Skip deliveries
- Reschedule deliveries
- Edit meals

These changes must usually be made before a cutoff time (typically the day before delivery or up to 24–48 hours in advance).

Delivery Policy:
Meals are delivered daily to the address provided by the customer. Delivery times are estimates and may vary.

If the customer is not available at the delivery address, meals may be left at the door or reception area depending on the location and instructions.

Customers are responsible for properly storing meals after delivery.

In cases of delays due to external factors (such as weather), deliveries may be rescheduled or refunded/credited.

Refund Policy:
Calo generally does not provide refunds for subscription payments after cancellation.

In some cases:
- Missed deliveries or failed service may result in credits or refunds
- Cancelled orders may be refunded as credits instead of cash

Payment Policy:
Customers authorize Calo to charge their selected payment method automatically for recurring subscription fees.

Payments are typically charged in advance for the upcoming subscription period.

Additional fees (taxes, delivery fees) may apply.

Modification Policy:
Customers can modify their meals, delivery time, and preferences before a cutoff time (usually the day before delivery).

After the cutoff time, no changes can be made to that delivery.

Food Safety and Responsibility:
Customers are responsible for inspecting meals upon delivery.

Once delivered, customers are responsible for:
- Proper storage
- Safe consumption

Calo is not responsible for issues arising after delivery if meals are not handled properly.

Account and Usage Policy:
Customers must provide accurate account and delivery information.

Users must be at least 18 years old to place orders.

By using the app, customers agree to all terms and conditions.

Flexibility Policy:
Calo allows:
- Pausing subscriptions
- Skipping deliveries
- Changing meals

Customers can manage everything directly through the app.

There are no long-term contracts, and subscriptions can be adjusted based on user needs.
